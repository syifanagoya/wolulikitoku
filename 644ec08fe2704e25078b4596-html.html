What's known about Shakira and Britney Spears attending the Met Gala 2023?

April 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Expectations are growing for the Met Gala 2023, which will be held on Monday 1 May at the Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York. This event brings together the world of fashion, music and the most important celebrities on the planet. At the head of this is the director of Vogue USA, Anna Wintour and for this edition she is planning some surprises.

Anna Wintour is one of the most powerful women in the industry, and she is also in charge of personally choosing the guests. This year there could be a big surprise, such as the presence of Shakira and Britney Spears, together with the designer Donatella Versace.

All that is known so far is that such a rumor could hold substance. First of all, Spears is a good friend of Donatella's, who was in charge of making the dress that Britney married Sam Asghari in.

As for Shakira, the Colombian is currently one of the female stars of the year, with her musical successes, which have earned her the award for Woman of the Year 2023 at the Billboard Latin Women in Music Awards.

If Shakira and Britney Spears appear together it would be a major media boost to the gala. In this year's theme, Wintour decided that Karl Lagerfeld, the creative director of Chanel, who died in 2019, and who did so much for fashion as one of its most influential representatives, should take center stage.
