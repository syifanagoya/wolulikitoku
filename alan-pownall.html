One of the outstanding personalities who has been underrated despite being a sensational talent is Alan Pownall. The talented singer/songwriter's sound owes more to the melodic guitar pop of early solos of the Kinks and Paul McCartney than the thriving nu-folk scene. 

So let's learn more about the musician and know more about his life.

Early Life

Alan Pownall was born on 30th December 1986 in the United Kingdom as the full name Stephen Alan Fletcher Pownall. He attended Windlesham House School and later went to Shiplake College.

Born to a barrister father, and a housewife mother, he has a very keen interest in the world of fashion. Previously worked for the French designer Roland Mouret for two years, he dropped out of the course after realizing music was his calling.

Know About Alan Pownall and Gabriella Wilde's Wedding 

The lead singer of the electro band Pale is a married man. He met his love life, Gabriella Wilde, back in 2009 at that moment both couples were in the struggling phase of their career. 

After dating for more than four years, Alan proposed to the beautiful Wilde whose response was obviously Yes. Being known each other for so much longer period of time, they seem very understanding of each other despite having separate careers and interests.

The beautiful couple shared wedding vows on 13th September 2014 in the beautiful Tellaro, Liguria, Italy where their family and friends were seen as the bridegroom's guests. Cressida Bonas who is the half-sister of Gabriella and ex-girlfriend of Prince Harry also attended the wedding ceremony. 

Father of Three Children

Alan and Wilde in now been married for more than a decade. Despite having a much extended nuptial relationship, they still seem very much deep in love with each other today. The decade-long relationship has led the pair to share three beautiful children.

They welcomed their first son Sasha Blue Pownall on 3rd February 2014. Fans from all over the world congratulate the beautiful pair. Interestingly, Gabriella was pregnant with Sasha while he married Alan. 

After just two years in 2016, the pair were blessed with their second son, Shiloh Silva Pownall. And Later in October 2019, they gave birth to their third son and named him Skye Pownall.

As of now, their sons are adorable growing boys respectively. The pair are taking care of their sons very well despite being both of them very much busiest personalities. During their holidays and weekends, they are often seen traveling to various places with the whole family.
