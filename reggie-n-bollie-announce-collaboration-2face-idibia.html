Reggie 'N' Bollie Announce Collaboration With 2Face Idibia

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Ghanaian duo Reggie ‘N’ Bollie stole the heart’s of the nation when they came second during the 2015 series of The X Factor where they were mentored by then judge Cheryl. But while they have built strong family and career roots in Britain the pair have never forgotten their African roots.

Earlier this month the lovable pair were named The Best UK Music Artist Award at the annual International Achievement Recognition Awards, where they received a record amount of votes.

We still can't believe we won The Best UK Music Artist Award @IARA_Awards Massive thank you to our fans for voting ???#grateful #winteam pic.twitter.com/YdZiCiKfrM

— Reggie n Bollie (@ReggieNBollie) September 3, 2017

And back in the summer Reggie ‘N’ Bollie were appointed as Tourism Ambassadors for Ghana in the UK and Europe by The Ministry of Tourism, Arts and Culture.

We're very honoured to be nominated by Ghana ministry of tourism as ambassadors in UK n Europe.What a privilege for us to represent Ghana?? pic.twitter.com/bqAvOrCTR2

— Reggie n Bollie (@ReggieNBollie) June 4, 2017

And now the Hip-Hop duo are celebrating their West African heritage by teaming up with Nigerian superstar 2Face Idibia aka 2 Baba. The eagerly anticipated Afrobeats collab ‘All The Way Up’ was announced by Reggie ‘N’ Bollie yesterday.

We're super excited to announce our Afrobeats collab with the legendary @official2baba .
Coming Out On Friday 15/09/2017#AllTheWayUp #Fire pic.twitter.com/A8j5SZn49S

— Reggie n Bollie (@ReggieNBollie) September 10, 2017

The sure-fire banger will be released on Friday, September 15th via the boys’ independent record label F.R.O.D Music.
